Building the Future Together

The Taskforce for Research Advancement in Artificial Intelligence and Cardiology (TRAIN-Cardio) is the largest consortium of cardiovascular AI researchers. With participation from researchers at over 20 institutions across the US, Canada, Korea, and China. TRAIN-Cardio regularly collaborates on large-scale validation studies and aims to advance the field of cardiovascular artificial intelligence.

Numerous collaborations through TRAIN-Cardio have resulted in impactful publications, including some of the largest multisite validations for ECGs, echocardiograms, and coronary catheterizations as diagnostic modalities.
