Michelle Castillo

Michelle Castillo is a dedicated Clinical Research Manager at Columbia University Irving Medical Center, where she plays a pivotal role in advancing cardiac imaging research. With over four years of clinical research experience, Michelle collaborates closely with principal investigators to refine research protocols, streamline data collection, and lead participant recruitment. Her work ensures accurate and comprehensive data management, supporting high-quality research outcomes.

Holding a Bachelor of Science in Physics from Montclair State University, Michelle is currently earning her Master of Science in Bioethics from Columbia University. Her academic and professional journey demonstrates a strong commitment to aligning ethical guidelines with organizational research objectives. With a passion for leveraging science and medicine to improve patient outcomes, Michelle strives to advance ethical research practices and cultivate lasting impacts within the field of healthcare research.
