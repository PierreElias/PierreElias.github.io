Our Focus

Deep learning will fundamentally change how we order, acquire, and review medical imaging over the next five years. We aim to build a world-leading center that develops, validates, and deploys deep learning technologies for earlier disease detection.

  • Learn More

Our Projects

  • ValveNet

    Detecting valvular heart disease from electrocardiograms

  • LVHnet

    Using chest x-rays to find heart failure patients before they develop symptoms

  • Cardiac Amyloidosis Discovery

    Detecting an underdiagnosed form of heart failure from electrocardiograms and echocardiograms

  • IntroECG

    Our tutorial on everything you need to start doing machine learning work on electrocardiograms

  • Antiracist AI

    Using AI models to correct for referral bias in clinical care

  • TRAIN-Cardio

    Our collaboratory network, the Taskforce for Research Advancement of Artificial Intelligence in Cardiology
